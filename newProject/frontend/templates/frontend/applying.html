ONLINE APPLICATION PROCESS:

Thank you for choosing a property offered by Purple Housing as your new home. In order to make the application process easier, please follow the directions below. WHEN MULTIPLE APPLICANTS APPLY FOR A PROPERTY, EACH APPLICANT IS EVALUATED SEPARATELY AND EACH MUST MEET THE QUALIFYING CRITERIA. The Property Owner(s) have the right of final approval of applications.

Each prospective tenant and occupant 18 years of age or older must submit a separate application (including each spouse if married). Cosigners and Guarantors must also submit a complete application.

The following Application Agreement will be signed by all applicants prior to signing a lease contract. While some of the information below may not yet be applicable to your situation, some provisions may become applicable prior to signing a lease contract. In order to continue with this online application, you will need to review the Application Agreement carefully and acknowledge that you accept its terms.

1. Application Fee: With your application, you must submit the nonrefundable application fee listed below for EACH APPLICANT and cosigner except that only one application fee is required for married couples. This fee may be paid WITH A CASHIER’S CHECK, MONEY ORDER, CERTIFIED CHECK, OR CREDIT CARD THROUGH OUR ONLINE APPLICATION. NO PERSONAL CHECKS OR CASH for the nonrefundable application fee. If you pay online with a credit card, $5 service fee per transaction will be assessed. We cannot process any applications until we have the funds in our office.

2. Application Deposit: If you want to have the property taken off the market for lease and held exclusively for you after you have been approved, you may do so by submitting an application deposit in CERTIFIED FUNDS in the amount of one month’s lease amount with your application. Otherwise, the property will continue to be marketed until you have executed a lease agreement. The application deposit is not a security deposit. If you are not approved, it will be refunded. If you are approved, it may be retained by us as liquidated damages if you fail to sign the lease within 3 days of application approval or withdraw your application.

3. Notification: We will notify you whether you’ve been approved within 7 days after the date we receive a completed Application. Any notice we give you or your co-applicant is considered notice to all co-applicants, and any notice from you or your co-applicant is considered notice from all co-applicants.

Our receipt of this application is consent only to this application agreement. It does not bind us to accept the applicant or to sign the proposed Lease Contract.

Please see the Terms of Agreement for detailed rental qualifications and automatic application rejections.

LEASING QUALIFICATIONS

The following items are the leasing qualifications which each applicant must meet:

1. Income: You must verify that you have monthly income of at least three times the amount of monthly rent by

providing one of the following:

a) A copy of your two previous year’s tax returns;
b) Copies of your previous year's W-2 and verification of your current year's income;
c) Copies of your previous six months’ bank statements indicating a stable income source; or
d) A letter from your attorney or CPA stating the following: “I/We certify that [applicant’s name] meets the income

qualifications for this property".

2. Employment: You must have current, verifiable employment and two years’ employment history unless self employed. An applicant who does not meet all requirements (such as a full time student) will require a cosigner or guarantor who meets all requirements.

3. Credit History: We will process a credit report for each applicant. Your credit history must meet our predetermined credit rating. Exceptions for medical reasons or prior bankruptcy will be considered.

4. Rental History: You must provide three years of verifiable residency indicating no outstanding debt to the previous landlord(s). If you are breaking a lease with a current landlord, you must provide a written release.

5. Application Rejection: Your application may be rejected for any of the following;
a) Eviction by a prior landlord;
b) Outstanding debt or unpaid damages to a previous landlord;
c) Undisclosed criminal record, felony or misdemeanor; including currently pending charges;
d) Being registered as a sex offender;
e) Conviction of a crime of a violent or abusive nature;
f) Bankruptcy in the past 2 years that has not been discharged/released;
g) Invalid social security number;
h) Outstanding federal tax lien, the repayments terms for which are not current;
i) False or incomplete information on your application;
j) Incomplete application, including applicant signature;
k) Failure to pay the application fee at the time of application submittal.

Pet Policy: If the owner permits a pet, a Pet Deposit is required. The Pet Deposit is a minimum of $200.00 per pet, depending on the Landlord’s requirements. This deposit is paid in addition to the security deposit in the Lease Contract. The deposit is made part of the security deposit for all purposes. More than one pet, or pets weighing over 30 pounds, are approved on an individual basis and with the owner’s permission only.
